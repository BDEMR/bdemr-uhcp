About
Diabetic Association of Bangladesh (BADAS) and Weave Our Future (WOF), a foundation of Auchan International, is launching Universal Health Coverage Plan (UHCP), for Garment Workers. Through UHCP BADAS and WOF plans to provide healthcare service and health education to the garment workers.

Service Deliver Points:
  • Garment Workers may obtain services from a qualified doctor through Call Center, Ibrahim Healthline, 24 hours, seven days a week,
  • Inside the Factory
  • Naryangonj City Centre/Diabetic Association, Salimullah Road, Chashara, Naryangonj
  • Narayanganj Diabetic Hospital, Deobagh, Nagbari, Naryanganj

In case of Referrals for highly specialized service or highly specialized doctors:
  • BIRDEM General Hospital, Shahbagh and Segun Bagicha Dhaka
  • National Healthcare Network (NHN) hospitals
  • Ibarhim Cardiac Hospital and Research Institute (ICHRI)
  • Bangladesh Institute of Health Sciences (BIHS) hospitals

UHCP, a joint collaboration of BADAS and WOF.

CTHealth and BDEMR are technological partners of UHCP.
